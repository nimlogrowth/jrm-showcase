About the Property

Dreamy Beachfront Townhouse: Your Perfect Seaside Escape

Welcome to our beautiful beachfront townhouse! Imagine a place where the beach is your backyard, offering you direct access to the sand and sea. This townhouse has been fully renovated to give you a fresh, modern feel while keeping that cozy vibe we all love.

Steps Away from the Beach:

Just open the door, and you’re on the beach! Enjoy the amazing views of the sea anytime you want. Whether it's watching the waves in the morning or catching a stunning sunset, the beach is always there for you.

Five Pools to Choose From:

Our community is pretty special, with five swimming pools waiting for you. Whether you want to swim laps or just chill in the water, there’s always a perfect spot for you to relax.

Everything Within Reach:

Need to grab something quick? We’re a short distance to a supermarket and other handy amenities. It’s all about making your stay as easy and enjoyable as possible.

Outdoor Fun Right at Home:

Our front patio is all set up for those who love being outdoors. With a BBQ area and a dining table, it’s the perfect place to enjoy meals with friends and family under the open sky.

Chill Vibes Everywhere:

This townhouse comes with multiple terraces that have a super relaxed vibe. Whether you’re catching some sun upstairs or lounging on the beanbags downstairs, you can always find a cozy spot to relax and enjoy the fresh sea air.

Comfort in Style:

With two bedrooms and two bathrooms, this place has enough room for everyone to stay comfortably. We’ve decked it out with stylish decor and top-notch finishes, making sure you feel right at home.

This beachfront townhouse is more than just a place to stay; it’s your own slice of paradise by the sea. Book your stay and start your day with the beach at your doorstep. Welcome to your ideal vacation spot.
