About the Property

Casa Agnes

A charming 3 bed Townhouse with private plunge pool!

Situated in the sought-after residential area of Guadalmina Baja, this lovely townhouse offers comfortable accommodation for 6 people, and is only 250m from the beach.

The open plan living area includes a fully equipped kitchen, a dining table and a cosy lounge area with TV, and a guest wc. There is a lovely, covered terrace with further seating and dining furniture, leading onto the patio and plunge pool. There are 4 sunbeds for your use.

Upstairs there are 3 double bedrooms: The Master has an ensuite bathroom, a TV and access to a terrace. The other 2 double bedrooms share a bathroom with a walk-in rain shower.

There is also a unique “Roman Bath” for your enjoyment.

There is aircon and wifi throughout the villa. There is a BBQ on the front patio, and there is parking for 1 car.

The location of Casa Agnes is ideal! A 2-minute walk to the beach, 4 minutes to a couple of restaurants and a 5-minute drive to the San Pedro Boulevard and all the parks, restaurants and shops of San Pedro. Also, there is a lovely Beach Club with tennis and padel courts, just 25m away and various golf courses within a 5km radius.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.

We look forward to welcoming you!
