About the Property

This frontline beach villa is located on one of the best beaches in Marbella.

This fantastic beach villa features an open-plan kitchen/dining/living area that opens out onto a terrace with an outdoor lounge and dining facilities, sunbeds and a private pool (with the option to heat for a weekly charge). As you are right on the beach, you will be able to wake up to the sea air, have coffee and breakfast watching the waves and then end the day sipping cocktails watching the sunset... Pure bliss!

The house is split over 4 floors, interconnected by an interior elevator. There are 6 double bedrooms, some having private sea-facing terraces, and the master suite featuring a stunning bathroom with a jacuzzi-bath.

A further very special feature is the “Exclusive Lounge” that can comfortably host 16 people. Additionally, the house is equipped with high-speed fiber, Sonos Music System, an alarm system, air conditioning with individual room control, under-floor heating in every room and TVs in every bedroom with international and Spanish channels.

On the 150m2 roof terrace you have an exercise area, an 8-seater jacuzzi and a bar and BBQ area. From here you have a 360-degree sea and mountain view – the perfect place to find inspiration, enjoy sundowners and alfresco dinners and to thoroughly immerse yourself in the wonderful Mediterranean lifestyle.

The Beach House is located in Elviria, 30 minutes’ drive from Malaga. This area boasts beautiful beaches and numerous acclaimed Chiringuitos, restaurants and bars, including Nikki Beach. There are supermarkets a plenty and you are only a short drive to Marbella and Puerto Banús to the west and 10 minutes’ drive to the bustling town of Fuengirola to the east.

Please note, from April to October there is a strict Saturday-Saturday, minimum 1-week booking policy.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.
