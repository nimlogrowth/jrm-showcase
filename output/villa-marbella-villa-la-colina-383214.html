About the Property

Villa La Colina – Your Private Paradise in the Heart of Nueva Andalucía

Imagine waking up in a peaceful oasis, just a short stroll from the buzzing Aloha strip in Marbella. At Villa La Colina, your days begin with sunlight pouring through the windows of your stylish en-suite bedroom, and the gentle sound of water lapping in the pool outside. Every corner of this contemporary villa invites you to relax, recharge, and truly savour your holiday.

Designed for both connection and comfort, Villa La Colina offers six beautifully appointed bedrooms — each with its own private en-suite bathroom. Four of these are conveniently located on the ground floor, offering direct access to the heart of the home. The master bedroom sits upstairs, a tranquil sanctuary with views over the pool and garden. For those seeking a little more privacy, a sixth bedroom is set apart in a charming separate guest apartment across the pool, complete with its own kitchenette — perfect for independent moments while still being part of the group.

Inside, the open-plan layout flows effortlessly from a sleek, fully equipped kitchen with island and bar seating, to a warm and inviting dining space where a handcrafted 12-seater wooden table takes centre stage. Just beyond, a sprawling 16-seater sofa stretches out in the lounge, facing wide glass doors that open to the covered terrace. Here, indoor and outdoor living blend seamlessly — whether you're watching a movie, sharing a bottle of wine, or simply enjoying the view of the pool glistening in the afternoon sun.

Step outside and you’ll discover the heart of the villa: a spectacular private pool with built-in underwater loungers and seating, surrounded by swaying palms, plush sunbeds, and a dreamy Bali bed that invites long naps or lazy afternoons with a book. The terrace leads to a lush lawn and a variety of unique spots to unwind — from a rooftop terrace with sweeping mountain views and a full outdoor kitchen with BBQ, to a hidden back patio where a large corner lounge, pizza oven, ping pong table, and basketball hoop set the scene for hours of fun.

As the sun sets, the villa transforms. The rooftop becomes a magical setting for dinner under the stars, while the poolside bar — complete with outdoor TV — is the perfect place to catch up on your favourite sports or enjoy a quiet evening drink. There’s even a bar overlooking the pool from the first floor, made for moments of quiet contemplation or conversation, while still staying close to the action.

The villa is tucked inside a secure, gated community in Nueva Andalucía’s prestigious Golf Valley. Just 500 metres away, you’ll find Marbella’s finest restaurants, beach clubs, boutique shops, and the vibrant energy of the Aloha strip. Everything you need — from a well-stocked supermarket to a top-tier sports club — is within easy reach.

Villa La Colina is more than just a place to stay — it’s a space to live, laugh, and make unforgettable memories with those who matter most. Whether you're lounging in the sun, cooking together in the outdoor kitchen, or enjoying the vibrant Marbella nights, this villa offers the perfect blend of serenity and sophistication.

Please note, to maintain the peaceful and luxurious environment, we welcome families and couples only. Groups aged under 30, or those planning stag or hen parties, are not permitted. However, if you feel Villa La Colina is the right fit for your group, we invite you to reach out and tell us more about yourselves — we’re always happy to consider special requests.
