About the Property

This absolutely breath-taking 4-bed Villa boasts amazing views over one of the best golf courses around stretching all the way down to the lovely shimmer of the Mediterranean Sea and ticks all the boxes for a perfect family holiday!

Villa Calma sits snug in the exclusive Valle Romano area in the hills overlooking Estepona town, one of the most desired locations due to its great golf course and serene ambience.

A new build with an exquisite attention to detail. An optionally heated (at an extra fee) infinity pool, lovely gardens, and an abundance of natural light– this is the perfect retreat for a large family or group of friends looking to relax and unwind in luxurious comfort and privacy.

This spectacular property is beautifully crafted with the trendy and modern Scandinavian touch. It shows off clean and minimalist shades of blue tones to give a sense of cool and elegance to the home, and the vibrant artwork and ornaments add some flavour to the mix as well!

On the entry level you will be greeted by the stunning spiral staircase and long views beyond. Moving into the fully equipped kitchen which will let you explore your favourite recipes with ease and comfort, from there the kitchen flows effortlessly into the large and spacious living area, a dreamy open plan design combining the dining area as well.

The Master Bedroom is of course stunning. The bed sits parallel to the private terrace access, and you have the pleasure of waking up to the serene views as far as the sea to your right. The ensuite bathroom is just as pretty as the room and complements it perfectly.

Headed upstairs, you will find the 3 other bedrooms. All of these upper-level bedrooms are double beds and have private access to the upper terrace! One room is ensuite with a shower/bath and the other two bedrooms share a bathroom with a shower.

Villa Calma boasts 2 separate dining areas, the internal with seating for 6. The external with seating for 4. The terrace and outdoor area are the highlights of this Villa. The partially covered terrace has a variety of spaces to use to chill-out, sunbathe, or enjoy a meal. The pool is surrounded by the manicured gardens and there is a range of sunbeds for your enjoyment.

The home has various lounge areas for you to enjoy, whether it be inside or outside, you will be spoilt for choice.

The home has private parking for 2 cars, as well as parking right outside the gate of the home on the street. Further features include individual hot and cold AC, high-speed Wi-Fi connection, electric BBQ, ping-pong table, electronic darts board, and 24/7 security in the neighbourhood.

This truly is an outstanding property and would suit a family looking for peaceful holidays enjoying luxury and exceptional views.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.

Don’t forget to enquire about our concierge service! We look forward to welcoming you.
