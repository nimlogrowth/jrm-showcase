About the Property

Discover the epitome of Scandinavian elegance in Villa Pinot, a stunning 4-bedroom retreat nestled in the prestigious La Resina area, perfectly positioned between Marbella and Estepona.

This villa is more than just a home; it’s a sanctuary where modern sophistication meets Mediterranean charm, with a distinctive Scandinavian twist. The spacious, chef-designed kitchen is a centerpiece for those who appreciate the art of cooking. Its open-plan layout flows effortlessly into the living and dining areas, anchored by a beautiful fireplace, creating the perfect setting for cozy, intimate dinners.

Step from the living room onto the covered outdoor dining terrace, complete with a barbecue area—ideal for al fresco meals. The outdoor space feels like your own private oasis, with a sparkling swimming pool, lush gardens, and even a football goal for the kids to practice their penalty kicks.

The villa offers four beautifully appointed bedrooms and three bathrooms. Upstairs, the master suite features a private en-suite and a balcony lounge area, offering a serene spot for morning coffee. Two additional bedrooms share a bathroom on this floor. From here, ascend to the rooftop terrace, where expansive views stretch over the golf course towards Africa and the iconic Rock of Gibraltar—an idyllic backdrop for evening sundowners. The lower level houses another bedroom with its own private bathroom.

Situated just a short drive from the vibrant town centers of Marbella and Estepona, world-class golf courses, and stunning beaches, Villa Pinot offers the perfect blend of seclusion and convenience—your ideal holiday destination.

Please note, we welcome families and couples only. We kindly ask that no groups under 30, hen, or stag parties make bookings. However, if you fall outside these criteria but are interested in booking, please reach out with details about your group, and we’ll be happy to consider your request.

We look forward to welcoming you to Villa Pinot for a truly unforgettable getaway!
