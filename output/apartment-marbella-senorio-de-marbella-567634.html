About the Property

Located in the prestigious Señorío de Marbella urbanisation within Lomas de Marbella Club, this charming 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom apartment offers a peaceful retreat in the heart of Marbella’s Golden Mile. Set within a beautifully maintained complex designed to resemble a traditional Andalusian village, the setting is as picturesque as it is serene—complete with cobbled walkways, whitewashed architecture, and lush greenery throughout.

Inside, the apartment is bright, welcoming, and well-appointed for a comfortable stay. Both bedrooms are generously sized, and the two bathrooms ensure privacy and convenience for families or small groups. Whether enjoying a morning coffee on the terrace or winding down after a day in town, the home strikes a perfect balance between relaxation and style.

Señorío de Marbella is known for its outstanding community facilities, including multiple swimming pools, tennis and padel courts, ping pong tables, a jacuzzi, and a playground for children. The gated complex is secure and family-friendly, offering everything you need for both active days and laid-back afternoons.

Positioned just a short stroll from Puente Romano, The Forum, and the beach, this apartment boasts one of Marbella’s most sought-after locations. Enjoy world-class dining, shopping, and beach clubs at your doorstep, while coming home to a peaceful, village-like oasis—this is Golden Mile living at its finest.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.
