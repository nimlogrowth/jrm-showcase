About the Property

Welcome to Villa Cortes, an exquisite haven of luxury nestled between El Paraiso and the prestigious Atalaya Golf Club. This recently refurbished 4-bed villa is a sun-drenched sanctuary, perfect for families seeking an unforgettable retreat. With 400m2 of carefully crafted living space and a sprawling 1,200m2 plot, Villa Cortes is more than a home – it's a lifestyle.

Step into the heart of this sunlit escape, where a large open-plan kitchen and living area beckon with the promise of conviviality. Boasting brand new Siemens appliances, a wine fridge, and a Ninja air fryer, the kitchen is a culinary haven where every meal is an experience. Gather around the expansive island with six bar stools, ideal for both cooking and socializing. The living area features a plush sofa and a reclining accent chair, creating a cozy retreat for movie nights or simply unwinding.

With 4 bedrooms, each accompanied by its own ensuite and private terrace, Villa Cortes offers the epitome of comfort. The main floor hosts a bright and spacious bedroom with flexible sleeping arrangements and a ensuite, providing captivating sea and mountain views. The top floor introduces a master suite with a super king bed, double-sink ensuite, and a private terrace. Another lavish bedroom on this level is adorned with a queen bed, smart TV, and a terrace offering the best views of the sea and mountains.

The lower level surprises with a 4th bedroom, a gaming/cinema area equipped with a PlayStation 4 and a 75-inch TV, and a convenient laundry room. The gaming area is a perfect retreat for both kids and adults, while the laundry room features an extra fridge and chest freezer for added convenience.

Outdoors, Villa Cortes invites you to indulge in a private paradise. The main terrace, facing the pool, has been fully reformed and features a large ceramic dining table, a BBQ, and a comfortable lounge area. The heated swimming pool, surrounded by sun loungers, beckons for a refreshing dip, while a 12ft trampoline, swing ball, and goal posts keep children entertained.

The villa also boasts a home gym, table tennis/ping pong table, and an outdoor WC with a shower.

Immerse yourself in the lavish lifestyle of the new golden mile, strategically located in the Golden Triangle between Marbella, Estepona, and Benahavis. Within 5-20 minutes, you can reach the vibrant Puerto Banus and explore renowned golf courses like Marbella Club, Villa Padierna, and Los Flamingos.

Discover a world of convenience with local amenities within walking distance, supermarkets a short drive away, and renowned restaurants such as Los Ramos and the Argentinian Grill (Asador Tehuelche Grill) nearby. Enjoy leisurely walks towards Benahavis town, visit the Selwo Adventure Park just 10 minutes away, or bask in the pristine beaches of Marbella and Estepona.

Villa Cortes is more than a residence; it's a refined retreat where luxury and leisure harmonize, promising an unforgettable escape for discerning families.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.

We look forward to welcoming you.
