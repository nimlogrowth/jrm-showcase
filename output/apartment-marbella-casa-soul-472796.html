About the Property

A brand new 3 bedroom apartment with Mountain & Sea Views! Introducing Casa Soul, a lovely retreat nestled within the brand new Soul Marbella complex, perfectly positioned near the Santa Clara Golf Club in the eastern greater Marbella area. This adorable 3-bedroom, 2-bathroom apartment offers a harmonious blend of contemporary luxury and modern styling. As you step inside, you are greeted by an ambiance of tranquility and sophistication. The spacious living area is soaked in natural light, adorned with elegant furnishings, and offers seamless access to the expansive terrace, where panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea and the majestic mountains surrounding Marbella await. The master bedroom with its en-suite bathroom provides ample comfort, while the two additional bedrooms share a bathroom between them allowing for plenty of space for guests or visitors. Each room is meticulously designed to create a sense of relaxation and refinement and all have wall-mounted SMART TV’s for your entertainment. Beyond the allure of the apartment itself, Casa Soul sits snug within the Soul Marbella complex, where residents have access to an array of world-class amenities. Immerse yourself in the ultimate relaxation at the spa, take a refreshing dip in the indoor or outdoor pools, or maintain your wellness routine at the state-of-the-art private gym. The social area with a café is the perfect spot to unwind and connect with neighbors and friends, and the complex ices its cake with a plethora of communal spaces, walking paths and gardens. For golf enthusiasts, the complex neighbours the Santa Clara Golf Club which ensures endless opportunities to enjoy the sport against the backdrop of stunning scenery. Casa Soul embodies the essence of luxury living in Marbella, offering an unparalleled lifestyle of comfort, convenience, and sophistication. A truly remarkable residence. Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you. We look forward to welcoming you!
