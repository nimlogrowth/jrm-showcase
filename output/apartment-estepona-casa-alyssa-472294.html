About the Property

Stylish Beachfront Haven in the Heart of Estepona

Welcome to our newly built, stylish beachfront apartment, situated right on the bustling boardwalk in the heart of Estepona. This gem offers the ultimate in luxury and convenience, making it the perfect getaway for those who want to experience both the vibrant life of Estepona as well as ultimate relaxation.

Directly on the Estepona Boardwalk: Step outside, and you're immediately immersed in the lively atmosphere of Estepona's boardwalk. With the beach, a plethora of restaurants, bars, a gym, shops, and more just outside your door, every day is an opportunity for a new adventure. Sports enthusiasts will delight in the nearby volleyball pitch and football goals on the beach.

Breathtaking Views: Enjoy south-facing views that promise stunning sunsets and abundant sunshine all afternoon. Whether you're inside looking out or soaking up the atmosphere on your terrace, the views of the sea and boulevard are nothing short of spectacular.

Elegant Living Space: The apartment features one bedroom with an en-suite bathroom with shower, combining prime quality construction, stylish design, and modern comfort. The open kitchen layout ensures you're always part of the conversation, making cooking a social affair. There is a further guest wc.

Wake Up to Sea Views: The master bedroom is your personal retreat, offering breathtaking sea views right from your bed. Start each day with the tranquil sights and sounds of the sea.

Versatile Terrace: Our terrace boasts incredible views and comes equipped with a breakfast table and a cozy lounge corner, perfect for enjoying Estepona's beautiful climate. In the winter, close off the space with glass curtains to keep it warm and inviting.

Rooftop Infinity Pool and More: Elevate your stay with access to a luxurious rooftop infinity pool, where the views are as endless as the pool itself. The rooftop also features a jacuzzi for the cooler months, gym equipment for workouts with a view, and a small play area for kids.

Perfectly Located: With the beach at your doorstep and the heart of Estepona all around you, there’s no need for a car. Everything you could want or need is within walking distance, providing the perfect blend of beach relaxation and urban excitement.

Convenient Parking: No worries about parking, as the apartment includes one parking spot in the garage, adding an extra layer of convenience to your stay.

This beachfront apartment is more than a place to stay—it's a lifestyle choice for those who seek the best of Estepona’s beachfront living, combined with luxury and modern convenience. Welcome to your Estepona escape!

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.
