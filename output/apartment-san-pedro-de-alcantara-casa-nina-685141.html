About the Property

A beautifully renovated 3-bedroom townhouse with a comfortable roof-top terrace, ideally located just a short stroll from the beach.

This elegant townhouse has been fully refurbished and features a brand-new kitchen and modern bathrooms throughout. Styled in calming neutral tones, the property offers three comfortable bedrooms accommodating up to 6 guests, along with an exceptional roof terrace complete with BBQ facilities — perfect for relaxed holiday living in Marbella.

On the entrance level, you’ll find a bright open-plan living and kitchen area. The kitchen is fully equipped with high-quality appliances and also features a built-in wine cooler, perfect for keeping your favourite bottles chilled. The living space offers comfortable seating for six and a large Smart TV, with Smart TVs also available in the bedrooms so guests can enjoy Netflix and other streaming services. Sliding doors lead out to a generous terrace with an outdoor dining area and cosy lounge seating, providing direct access to the beautifully maintained communal gardens and a large swimming pool.

The upper floor hosts all three bedrooms. The master bedroom includes an en-suite bathroom with a rain shower, TV, and a private balcony. The second bedroom features a double bed, and the third bedroom also offers a double bed. These two rooms share a modern bathroom with a walk-in shower.

At the top of the house, the impressive roof terrace awaits. It boasts a covered outdoor kitchen with gas BBQ, sink, and a high dining table with seating, sun loungers, an outdoor shower, and additional seating areas — all overlooking the peaceful communal grounds.

Additional features include air conditioning and WiFi throughout, Smart TVs in all bedrooms and the living room, as well as on-street parking and a garage for up to two cars within a secure 24/7 guarded area.

Located in the gated community of Casa Guadalvillas, the property is just 200 metres from the beach, restaurants, and local amenities. A pleasant 15-minute walk brings you to San Pedro Boulevard, known for its lively atmosphere, restaurants, shops, playgrounds, and charming Old Town. Puerto Banús is only a 5-minute drive away, with Marbella and Estepona reachable within 10–15 minutes.

Please note that we do not accept hen or stag parties, and groups under 30 are not permitted unless approved in advance.

We look forward to welcoming you!
