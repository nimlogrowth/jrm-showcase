About the Property

Villa Sueña Blanca has been specifically designed for the perfect holiday experience. The villa is completed to high standards and features 7 bedrooms (16 sleeps), 6 bathrooms, amazing sea and golf views, an infinity pool, a 10p Jacuzzi, an outdoor kitchen, a pool table room, and a bar area. Walking distance to golf and only 5 minutes drive to Puerto Banus and its lovely beaches. You will wish you would never have to leave!

The property has a very modern and bright feel to it. It boasts great views from various levels both of the Mediterranean sea as well as one of Marbella’s finest golf courses; Los Naranjos.

The outdoor area around the villa offers different spots to sit and relax or dine while you enjoy views of the sea, garden, and the swimming pool. The swimming pool is an infinity pool from which you can enjoy the great sea views and Marbella’s surrounding mountains. If desired there is an option to heat the pool (at an additional weekly cost).

As an alternative to the stunning infinity pool, there is a 10-person Jacuzzi on the rooftop of the property. From here you have 360-degree panoramic views. No better place to enjoy the fine sunsets.

Also outside, there is a bar with 2 refrigerators, freezers, a wine cooler, an ice cube machine, a sink with normal and drinking water, a hob, a dishwasher, and a grill station. The area is accompanied by a large lounge area. For those who like to cook up the BBQ under the sun, this is the ideal area.

For those that would also like to escape the sun at times, no stress, there is a pool table room with a large screen for TV, movies or games, and a few exercise machines inside of the property!

The renowned “Puerto Banús”, is situated only a few minutes by car from the villa. This is Marbella’s seaport in which the most famous yachts moor. You can also sunbathe in one of its adjacent beaches, shop in the most exclusive boutiques, dine in one of the many restaurants, or have a drink in a trendy bar or club.

If you prefer golf as an activity, Los Naranjos Golf is just around the corner and there are 17 more luxurious golf courses within a 25km radius of the villa. There are 32 golf courses in the province of Malaga, which is why this region is also called the “Costa del Golf”.

Shortly said, with this villa we hope to offer you a great holiday experience never to forget, and look forward to welcoming you!

Please note, from April to October there is a strict Saturday-Saturday, minimum 1-week booking policy.
