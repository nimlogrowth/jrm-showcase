About the Property

Villa Hakuna Matata – Set within the serene enclave of La Alqueria, this exquisite 4-bedroom villa offers a blend of elegance and comfort, capturing captivating views of the golf course, La Concha mountain, and a glimpse of the Mediterranean Sea. The property’s design emphasizes light and space, with expansive windows framing the scenic landscape and flooding the interiors with natural light.Inside, the open-plan living and dining areas connect seamlessly to a well-appointed kitchen, ideal for hosting or relaxed evenings in. Each bedroom has been crafted as a private sanctuary, offering comfort and tasteful decor with easy access to views or the outdoor spaces.Outside, the villa’s terrace and private pool area provide an inviting spot to unwind, whether enjoying a morning coffee with mountain views or an evening swim. The landscaped gardens further enhance the sense of tranquility, making Villa Hakuna Matata not only a refined retreat but a perfect home to embrace the serene Costa del Sol lifestyle.Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.We look forward to welcoming you!
