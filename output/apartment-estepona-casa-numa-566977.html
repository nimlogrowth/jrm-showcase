About the Property

Welcome to Casa Numa! Step into stylish coastal living with this newly built, beautifully designed penthouse in the heart of Estepona. Flooded with natural light, the spacious open-plan layout blends modern comfort with a warm, relaxed ambiance—thanks to soft tones, textured walls, and natural wood accents throughout the interior. The living area opens directly onto an expansive terrace with panoramic views that stretch from the lush padel courts below to the sparkling Mediterranean Sea in the distance.

Perfect for entertaining or unwinding, the outdoor space features both lounge and dining zones under a sleek pergola, ideal for sunset cocktails or sunny brunches. The fully equipped kitchen boasts integrated appliances and a convenient breakfast bar, while the generously sized bedrooms offer plush bedding and garden or mountain views. The modern bathrooms are elegantly finished with walk-in showers and clean lines.

As part of a well-maintained community, residents enjoy access to a private gym and a sun-soaked swimming pool area complete with loungers. The complex is also pet friendly, so you can bring your furry companion along to enjoy the Mediterranean lifestyle with you!

All of this is located within walking distance of Estepona’s charming town centre, golden beaches, and vibrant port—making it a perfect home base for both relaxation and exploration.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.

We look forward to welcoming you.
