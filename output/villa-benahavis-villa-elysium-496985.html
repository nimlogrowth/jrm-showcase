About the Property

Luxurious Oceanview Villa with Infinity Pool in Marbella – Perfect for Unforgettable Getaways!

Welcome to your dream vacation destination! Nestled in the serene, gated community of El Madroñal, one of the most luxurious areas in Marbella, this exquisite 7-bedroom villa offers the ultimate in luxury and relaxation. Perfect for families, groups, or couples seeking a lavish retreat, our villa promises an unforgettable stay.

Villa Highlights:

Spectacular Views: Enjoy breathtaking views of the sea and valley from every corner of this newly built 1500 square meter villa, set on a sprawling 5000 square meter plot. The expansive windows and open-plan design ensure you’re always connected to the stunning surroundings.

Infinity Pool & Firepit Lounge: Dive into the very large swimming pool, featuring a firepit seating area and additional poolside seating. Whether you’re swimming, sunbathing, or stargazing, this space is designed for ultimate relaxation.

Outdoor Entertainment Areas: The villa boasts multiple outdoor entertainment spaces, including a stylish BBQ area with a Green Egg, al fresco dining setup, and luxurious lounge seating. Perfect for BBQs, sunset cocktails, and memorable gatherings.

Elegant Interiors: Step inside to find spacious, elegantly designed interiors with high ceilings and floor-to-ceiling windows. The living area, dining space, and modern kitchen are all meticulously decorated to provide comfort and style.

Comfortable Bedrooms: The villa features seven well-appointed bedrooms, each with its own ensuite bathroom. Wake up refreshed and ready to explore or relax.

🍽️ Gourmet Kitchen: The state-of-the-art kitchen is fully equipped with high-end appliances, perfect for preparing delicious meals. Enjoy your culinary creations at the large dining table with views of the ocean.

Luxurious Bathrooms: Each bathroom is designed with luxury in mind, featuring modern fixtures, spacious showers, and premium toiletries.

Private Gym & Cinema: Stay active in the private gym or unwind in the amazing cinema room, perfect for family movie nights or catching up on the latest films.

Kids' Paradise: With an outdoor kids' playground, a boys' room for four, and a girls' room for four, this villa is ideal for families with children.

Experience the epitome of luxury and tranquility at our stunning oceanview villa in El Madroñal. Whether you’re seeking adventure or relaxation, our villa provides the perfect backdrop for your dream vacation. Don’t miss out – book your stay today and create unforgettable memories in this paradise retreat!
