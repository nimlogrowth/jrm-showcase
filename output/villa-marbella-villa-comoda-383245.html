About the Property

Spectacular 4-bedroom villa on a large corner plot with amazing sea views. Modern and stylish interiors with designer finishes throughout, featuring an open-plan kitchen with induction hob, oven, microwave, freezer, dishwasher, juicer, and all utensils. Four bedrooms each with double beds (bedroom 4 also has a bunk bed), one bathroom with bathtub, two with shower, and a guest toilet. The outdoor area includes a 150 m² terrace, private heated saltwater infinity pool, landscaped fenced garden, barbecue area, and outdoor shower. Additional features include a fireplace, safe, dryer, balcony, alarm system, ping pong table, and air conditioning individually controlled per room with central heating. Secured parking available.
