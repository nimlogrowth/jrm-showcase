About the Property

Welcome to Casa Pentique, a charming townhouse cuddled in the heart of Urbanización Señorío de Cortés, a lovely and tranquil residential neighborhood in Nueva Atalaya offering a delightful array of amenities such as a community pool, children's play area and tennis & Padel courts for the whole family to enjoy.

Immerse yourself in the essence of modern comfort and timeless Andalusian charm as you step into this beautifully refurbished home. Designed with a tasteful blend of modern Scandinavian flavours and traditional Andalusian elements, Casa Pentique exudes warmth and character at every turn.

Indulge in the pleasures of outdoor living with multiple terraces totalling a space of 65m2 and a private garden, providing ample space for relaxation and play. The gas barbecue on the outer terrace invites you to savor delicious meals under the Mediterranean sun or starry skies.

Inside, the home boasts a seamless fusion of living, dining, and kitchen areas, creating an inviting space for gatherings and quality time with loved ones. The kitchen is a masterpiece of modern design, equipped with top-of-the-line appliances and finished to perfection.

With three bedrooms, all of which are ensuite, Casa Pentique offers comfort and convenience for families seeking a memorable holiday experience.

Venture to the upper terraces and be captivated by breathtaking views of the Mediterranean Sea and majestic mountains, providing the perfect backdrop for relaxation and rejuvenation.

Conveniently located with easy access to the main highway, Casa Pentique offers effortless connectivity to Marbella City, Puerto Banus, and Estepona, while the charming town of San Pedro is just a stone's throw away.

Experience the best of all worlds with walking distance proximity to shops, restaurants, and amenities including golf courses such as El Campanario which also has a spa & gym next door, ensuring every moment of your stay is filled with convenience and enjoyment.

Escape to Casa Pentique and create cherished memories with your family amidst the enchanting landscape of Marbella. Your ideal family retreat awaits.

We look forward to welcoming you.
