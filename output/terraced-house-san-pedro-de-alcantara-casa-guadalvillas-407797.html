About the Property

A charming 3-bed Townhouse completely renovated, with a roof-top terrace, within walking distance to the beach.

This lovely Townhouse has been completely renovated with a new kitchen and bathrooms. Beautifully decorated in soft neutral tones, 3 bedrooms (sleeps 5) and a great roof-top terrace with BBQ, this is the perfect choice for your holidays in Marbella.

The entrance level hosts the open-plan living/kitchen area with a breakfast bar. The kitchen is brand new with good quality appliances. The lounge has seating for 6 and a large Smart TV. There is access to a sizeable terrace with outdoor dining table and a chill out lounge. From here you have direct access to the lovely communal areas with grassed areas and a very big swimming pool.

Upstairs you will find the 3 bedrooms. The Master has an ensuite bathroom with rain shower, a TV, and a small balcony. The second bedroom is a twin room. The 3rd bedroom has a single bed and a desk – perfect for remote working! These 2 bedrooms share a bathroom (with shower).

On the top level is the fabulous roof terrace that features a covered outdoor kitchen with a gas BBQ, sink and dishwasher. There is a high table and chairs where you can enjoy alfresco dining overlooking the lovely communal grounds. Around the corner there is an outdoor shower, sunbeds and a further seating area.

There is on-street parking (with 24/7 security) and aircon and wifi throughout.

Casa Guadalvillas is in a secure gated urbanization, 200 m from the beach and restaurants, and a 15 minute walk to the San Pedro Boulevard with its bars, restaurants and playgrounds, and supermarkets. San Pedro has a charming Old Town with a bustling evening vibe.

The location of Casa Guadalvillas is ideal for families, small groups and golfers with the beach, bars, restaurants and entertainment, and golf courses all within 1 kilometre. A 5 minute drive will get you to Puerto Banús, and in 10/15 minutes you could be in Estepona or Marbella.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.

We look forward to welcoming you!
