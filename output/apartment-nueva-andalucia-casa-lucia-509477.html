About the Property

Welcome to Casa Lucia! This 2-bedroom townhouse is located in the desirable Cerro Blanco urbanization in Marbella, combining modern design with Mediterranean charm. The property features a bright living room furnished with comfortable, neutral-toned seating, accented by blue chairs and a modern fireplace for added warmth. The space transitions seamlessly into a dining area with a glass-top table and a stylish light fixture.

The kitchen is equipped with high-quality appliances, ample counter space, and practical cabinetry, making it both functional and appealing for everyday use. The two bedrooms provide a comfortable setting with soft furnishings, large windows that allow natural light to flow in, and built-in mirrored wardrobes for added storage. The master bedroom overlooks the surrounding greenery, creating a peaceful environment. The bathrooms are updated with modern fixtures, including a walk-in shower and decorative elements for a clean, contemporary look.

A standout feature of the townhouse is the terrace, shaded by a yellow awning and ideal for outdoor relaxation or dining. This space offers views of the well-maintained gardens and the communal pool, as well as sea views, making it a great spot to enjoy the pleasant Marbella weather.

Cerro Blanco is known for its well-kept grounds and communal amenities, including landscaped gardens and a pool. The property is conveniently located close to Marbella’s town center, shops, restaurants, and beaches, making it an excellent option for those seeking both comfort and accessibility.

This townhouse is perfect for those looking for a blend of modern living and the relaxed lifestyle that Marbella offers.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only. No groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. If you fall outside this category but wish to book, please send us an enquiry with more information about yourself and your group so we can consider your request.

We look forward to welcoming you.
