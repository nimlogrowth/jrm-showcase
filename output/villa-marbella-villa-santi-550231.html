About the Property

Villa Santi is a beautifully appointed 5-bedroom, 4.5-bathroom villa ideally located in one of Nueva Andalucía’s most vibrant hotspots—just steps away from La Sala, Marbella, and Puerto Banús. Recently renovated and designed for effortless indoor-outdoor living, this stylish retreat offers privacy, comfort, and unbeatable access to the best dining, shopping, and beach clubs in the area.

Step inside to discover a bright and spacious interior, where neutral tones, modern finishes, and large windows create a relaxed, contemporary vibe. The newly renovated kitchen is fully equipped and designed for both function and flow—perfect for group dinners or relaxed breakfasts before a day out. Each of the five bedrooms is comfortably furnished, with soft lighting and sleek storage, while four bathrooms and a guest toilet offer total convenience for larger groups.

Outdoors, the villa shines as a social hub. A large private pool with optional heating takes centre stage, surrounded by sun loungers and shaded chill-out areas. The outdoor BBQ space invites al fresco meals with friends and family, while the garden’s privacy makes it feel like a peaceful oasis despite being just minutes from the action.

Whether you’re planning a laid-back poolside holiday or want to be close to Marbella’s lively scene, Villa Santi offers the perfect base—combining modern luxury with one of the best locations in town.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.
