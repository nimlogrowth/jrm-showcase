About the Property

Wake up to the sound of waves and uninterrupted sea views at this stunning 3-bedroom, 2-bathroom beachfront townhouse in the exclusive Casares del Mar community. Located on the peaceful shores of Casares Costa, this elegant home sits directly on the frontline beach, offering uninterrupted views of the Mediterranean and immediate access to the sand just steps from your door.

Inside, the property has been tastefully renovated with a clean, modern design. The separate kitchen is fully equipped and finished to a high standard, while the bright living area opens directly onto a spacious private terrace—perfect for al fresco dining or simply relaxing with panoramic sea views. Both bathrooms feature sleek rain showers, combining comfort with a touch of luxury.

The home comfortably sleeps up to seven guests across three bedrooms, making it ideal for families or small groups. One of the bedrooms includes both a double and single bed, offering flexibility for various sleeping arrangements. Each room is well-furnished and bathed in natural light, creating a calm and welcoming atmosphere throughout the house.

Set within a secure gated complex, Casares del Mar offers residents access to two communal swimming pools and beautifully maintained gardens. The location is peaceful yet well-connected, with charming coastal towns like Estepona and Casares just a short drive away, as well as local beach bars, golf courses, and supermarkets nearby. This is coastal living at its finest—combining style, comfort, and unbeatable sea views.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.
