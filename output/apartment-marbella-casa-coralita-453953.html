About the Property

Welcome to your coastal haven nestled in the heart of luxury, where sun, sand, and sophistication converge. JustRentMarbella is delighted to present this exceptional 2-bedroom groundfloor apartment, flawlessly renovated and poised just a mere 50 meters away from the golden shores of Puerto Banús Beach.

Casa Coralita boasts a modern but soulful feel by offering soft, neutral tones, subtle downlighting, and contemporary furnishings with wooden accents and intricate patterns all over the home, making it a unique and fresh space for your holidays in Marbella.

Upon entering, the fully equipped kitchen is to the right which extends through an open plan design into the living room with a lounge area, Smart TV, and a dining table, that further leads to the comfortable and cozy terrace with views into the gardens and communal swimming pool.

The terrace has a dining table as well as a coffee lounge/chill-out area.

The Master bedroom has a large bed and a bathroom beside it with a rain shower. There is another bedroom which holds two large single beds that also has its own bathroom.

There is aircon and Wi-Fi throughout the home, private parking, and ground-floor access to the apartment.

The gated urbanisation, with 24hr security, boasts grassed areas, and communal swimming pools, and is quite literally a 20-second stroll to the sands of the beach and the promenade.

There are plenty of bars and restaurants nearby and a supermarket within walking distance. The bustling area of Puerto Banus is just a short walk away and you can even head out and walk along the entirety of the paseo along the beach and back.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.

We look forward to welcoming you.
