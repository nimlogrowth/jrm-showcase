About the Property

A breathtaking 4-bed family villa located in the heart of Nueva Andalucia, second line to the famous golf course “Los Naranjos”.

This elegant, fresh, and contemporary villa is situated in a very privileged location of Marbella, a short distance from all amenities. Villa Orion has it all for the perfect holiday!

Stocked with state-of-the-art fixtures and fittings and filled with fantastic features such as 4 ensuite bedrooms, a cinema room with a kid’s area, and sublime views stretching across the golf courses and mountains all the way down to the Mediterranean Sea, this is the perfect choice to come and enjoy the luxurious Marbella lifestyle with your every whim catered for.

The ground floor offers an open-plan living space with a fully equipped kitchen with a breakfast bar, a dining table, and a lounge area with a large Smart TV. Large sliding doors give access to the wonderful terrace, garden, and pool.

The villa has good-sized lawned gardens around the private pool and other sections of the home. There are sunbeds and umbrellas as well as an outdoor chill-out area on the covered section of the terrace. There is a gas BBQ on the terrace with an outdoor dining area. The garden is surrounded by hedges and palm trees, providing privacy and tranquillity.

Inside, the soft-toned furnishings, subtle and smooth lines, top-of-the-range fixtures, and good use of lighting and natural light provide this villa with a luxurious, spacious, and relaxing ambiance. The bedrooms are comfortably spread out around the home, 2 upstairs, one on the entry-level, and one downstairs.

The highlight of Villa Orion is not only its panoramic views but also its entertainment ability. A Gaming/Cinema room, and an amazing workout area, with a feature rock-climbing wall, complete the home.

The upper-level terraces all have various seating arrangements providing an opportunity to kick back and enjoy the sun with a cup of coffee or a book.

Additional features include high-speed Wi-Fi throughout, air conditioning, and private parking spaces for 2 cars.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.

This luxury 5-bedroom villa promises to provide an unforgettable holiday experience. Don’t forget to enquire about our concierge services. We look forward to welcoming you!
