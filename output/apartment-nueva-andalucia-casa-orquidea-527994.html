About the Property

Casa Orquidea is a beautifully renovated 2-bedroom apartment located in the heart of Nueva Andalucía, offering a perfect blend of modern comfort and convenience. Situated within a gated community, this bright and stylish home is just a short walk from supermarkets, restaurants, and the beach, making it an ideal choice for those looking to enjoy everything Marbella has to offer.

The apartment features an open-plan living space with contemporary furnishings, a fully equipped kitchen, and a sleek, modern bathroom. Large windows bring in plenty of natural light, creating a welcoming and airy atmosphere. Both bedrooms are designed for relaxation, with comfortable beds and a minimalist aesthetic.

Outside, the complex boasts well-maintained gardens and a shared swimming pool, perfect for cooling off on warm days. With gated parking, air conditioning, high-speed WiFi, and easy access to top attractions, Casa Orquidea is a fantastic base for a relaxing and enjoyable stay on the Costa del Sol.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.
