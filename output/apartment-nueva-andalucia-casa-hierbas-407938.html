About the Property

Casa Hierbas offers you the perfect Marbella experience; Ideal location with the beach, bars and supermarket at walking distance, sea views and all to a fully renovated Scandinavian style!

Newly refurbished and decorated in a contemporary Scandinavian style, this fabulous 3 bedroom apartment has it all! Modern looks, comfortable living, a very large terrace overlooking the pool, and an ideal location with all necessities at walking distance.

The open-plan living area with a stylish lounge, smart TV, and fully fitted kitchen opens to the very spacious terrace that features a chill-out corner and dining area with a BBQ, all enjoying views over the communal grounds. There is an adjustable awning providing shade for alfresco dining in the summer.

There are 3 double bedrooms, The Master has a double bed and ensuite bathroom with rain shower. The other 2 bedrooms are twin rooms and share a bathroom with a shower. All bedrooms have access to the lovely terrace.

Further features include underfloor heating in the bathrooms, hot/cold AC, Apple TV, and a washer/dryer.

The complex enjoys a peaceful atmosphere, lovely gardens, and a large communal swimming pool. Parking is on the street.

The location of the apartment is perfect, close to it all yet in a quiet area. Puerto Banús is only a 15 minutes’ walk away, so is the beach. You have a few restaurants and bars within 5 minutes walk, along with a supermarket. Marbella Old Town is 10 minutes’ drive away, and you have 4 world-class golf courses less than 5 minutes’ drive away (Aloha, Las Brisas, Los Naranjos, and La Quinta).

From its convenient location in Nueva Andalucía, you have quick and easy access to so many of the Costa del Sol’s sought-after attractions, beaches, avant-garde cuisine, mountain-top white-washed villages, and more.

Please note, if it’s a party you are looking for, then this apartment is not for you. This property is ideal for families and couples only, non-smokers, without pets. So if you are looking for a comfortable, relaxed, sunlit haven and want to experience the best of the Costa del Sol in style, Casa Hierbas is a perfect choice.

We look forward to welcoming you!
