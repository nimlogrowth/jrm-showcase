About the Property

Stunning New 4-Bed Villa with Sea Views in Banus Heights!

Welcome to Villa Banus Heights, a brand-new luxury villa situated in the beautiful Banus Heights development. This exquisite property offers modern and stylish interiors, designer furniture, spacious bedrooms, and fabulous living and entertainment areas, making it the perfect choice for an unforgettable holiday in Marbella.

A large open-plan living, dining, and kitchen area coupled with full-size picture windows opening out to the terrace, where you’ll find a beautiful pool and outdoor area perfect for relaxation. The seamless integration of indoor and outdoor spaces creates a harmonious flow, ideal for enjoying the warm Mediterranean climate.

The villa boasts 4 beautifully designed bedrooms, each with stunning sea views. The master and a second bedroom on the lower level feature private bathrooms, while the remaining two upstairs bedrooms share a third bathroom. The lower-level bedroom, with its direct access to the terrace, offers a perfect retreat for guests, allowing them to step out and enjoy the morning breeze or an evening dip in the pool.

The terrace is outfitted with luxurious furniture, including a chill-out lounge, a dining area with a wood-topped table, sunbeds for soaking up the sun, and a BBQ and outdoor dining area for al fresco dining.

Further features include a private garage with parking for 2 cars, underfloor heating throughout, air conditioning, high-speed WiFi, and an alarm system.

Villa Banus Heights is designed to meet the highest standards of luxury and comfort, ensuring a memorable stay for families and couples. The villa is conveniently located close to the vibrant Puerto Banus, Marbella’s beaches, golf courses, and an array of restaurants and bars. Everything you need for an unforgettable Marbella holiday is just minutes away.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only. No groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties are allowed. Should you fall outside of this category but still wish to book, please send us an enquiry with more details about yourself and your group.

We look forward to welcoming you to Villa Banus Heights.
