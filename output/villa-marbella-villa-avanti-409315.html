About the Property

-Villa Avanti-

The contemporary design, the prime location and the commanding views over the whole coastline turn Villa Avanti into one of a kind in Marbella. The property is offering the best in luxury living with voluminous rooms bathed in natural light through walls of windows and panoramic views from every corner of the house.

It could be the sophisticated vibe, the elegant interiors, heavenly gardens, magnetic views, irresistible pools, an extensive range of quality amenities or a mixture of some or all of these elements that makes this villa the crème de la crème of our range, the very best luxury villas currently available in the area.

Villa Avanti is a luxurious one of a kind Villa, guaranteed to fulfil the wishes of anyone’s greatest fantasy, located in the most sought-after urbanisation in Marbella’s most prime, luxurious areas. Sierra Blanca, Golden Mile, is one of the most exclusive residential areas of Marbella with majestic villas lining it’s quiet and secluded streets.

Sierra Blanca boasts the best location in Marbella, with beautiful ocean views, Mediterranean vegetation and authentic Andalusian Design. Our Villa is a 5 min drive to the Beach and city centre. This Luxury gated community has it all : 24hrs security. 2 minutes from Marbella with its local shops and amenities, 3 minutes from some of the Costa del Sol’s best beaches, privacy and exclusivity with luxurious villas lining it’s quiet and secluded streets, close to international schools and hospitals.

With sea views from every level, Villa Avanti also offers one of Marbella’s most iconic panoramic views, where you can relax in tranquillity whilst looking into the infinity of the Med.

Villa Avanti is built over four levels, with a luxurious design which will please anyone lucky enough to rent the property.

From the kitchen, to dining area, to the living room, to all the 6 bedrooms and 7 bathrooms, from the garden the pool and to the infinity pool, the style, the design and function is very consistent and harmonious .

This property seduces the senses from the moment you enter on its space, it welcomes you in with a double height entrance hall, open concept contemporary kitchen, fully fitted and equipped with top spec appliances and an informal dining area. There is also a beautiful reception room leading on to a large separate TV Room. In addition, there are 2 large en- suite bedrooms with walk in dressing areas and a guest toilet. The kitchen, lounge and TV Room all provide direct access to a covered terrace which offers an informal seating and dining area, this leads into the beautiful manicured tropical landscaped gardens with a large pool, jacuzzi and sunken poolside dining area, as well as a pool house with a bar and BBQ.

The first floor includes the master bedroom, with a stunning walk in dressing room, a bathroom with a walk-in rain shower, and separate luxurious standalone bath where you can watch TV whilst admiring magnificent view of the med, or keep warm in front of the state of the art fire. Leading on from the bedroom is a panoramic private terrace with a unique glass ceiling looking in to the rooftop pool like you were in a 007 movie. In addition, a further two-bedroom suites with separate walk in dressing rooms and private bathrooms are located on this level.

The lower ground level aka the ‘entertainment level’ features a spectacular wine cellar, professional gym, state of the art sauna and steam room, bar, games room, home cinema, 4 car garage and a fully fitted one-bedroom apartment which can also be used as staff quarters should this be required.

The rooftop entertainment area includes double sun-beds, dining area, bar, BBQ, chill sofas and a stunning infinity pool with views to the Med and La Concha mountain.

Villa Avanti has super-fast wifi, sonos music system and cable TV in all key areas.

Villa Avanti is perfect for family holidays, golf breaks and winter sun vacations. Enjoy the best panoramic views including Puerto Banús, Gibraltar and North Africa, the sea, the bay of Marbella, the beautiful pools, the tropical garden and the surrounding mountains.

This welcoming and refined villa features all the necessary elements for a successful holiday home. Villa is also only a short drive from the famous Golf Valley, a paradise for golfers, with some excellent golf courses nearby : Aloha, Las Brisas, Los Naranjos, La Quinta and Magna Marbella.

Villa Avanti leaves nothing to the imagination and we look forward to welcoming you into this beautiful and unique property.
