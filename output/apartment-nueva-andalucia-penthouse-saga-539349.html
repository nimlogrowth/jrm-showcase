About the Property

Welcome to Penthouse Saga, a bright and stylish 4-bedroom, 2-bathroom home located in the heart of Nueva Andalucía, within the highly sought-after gated community of La Maestranza. This welcoming apartment is designed for comfort, convenience, and relaxed Mediterranean living. Set in one of Marbella’s most popular residential zones, it offers a peaceful escape while still being just moments away from the vibrant energy of Puerto Banús, the beaches, and a host of top amenities.

The home features a thoughtfully laid out interior with tasteful, contemporary furnishings and a calm, airy ambience throughout. The spacious living and dining area is filled with natural light and opens directly onto a sunny terrace overlooking the community’s lush gardens—perfect for your morning coffee or evening drinks. The modern kitchen comes fully equipped with quality appliances and ample workspace, ideal for preparing meals at home whether you're hosting or enjoying a quiet evening in.

There are three well-appointed bedrooms offering comfortable bedding and built-in storage, with an extra fourth double bed on its own. The master bedroom features an ensuite bathroom and direct access to the terrace, creating a lovely private retreat. The guest bedrooms share a second family bathroom that includes both a bathtub and shower, offering flexibility for families and groups.

Set within La Maestranza, one of the most charming urbanisations in Nueva Andalucía, known for its security, Andalusian architecture, and beautifully maintained communal spaces. The complex features two swimming pools (including one heated) and peaceful landscaped gardens, making it a serene setting year-round.

Outside the gates, you’re just a short walk to supermarkets, local shops, pharmacies, cafés, and a wide range of excellent restaurants in the Aloha area. Golf enthusiasts will appreciate being surrounded by some of Marbella’s best courses, including Los Naranjos, Las Brisas, and Aloha Golf Club. The beaches and bustling marina of Puerto Banús are only a 5-minute drive away, while Marbella town and San Pedro are also easily accessible by car.

Whether you're looking for a relaxing home base, a golf holiday getaway, or a stylish spot to enjoy Marbella’s best offerings, Penthouse Saga delivers the perfect balance of location, charm, and functionality.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.
