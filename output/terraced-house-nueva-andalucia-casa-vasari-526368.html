About the Property

This Andalusian-style townhouse in Urbanización Alzambra, Nueva Andalucía, offers a seamless blend of Mediterranean charm and modern Scandinavian-inspired interiors. Neutral tones, soft textures, and elegant décor create a warm and inviting atmosphere throughout its 4 spacious bedrooms and 3 bathrooms. Each bedroom features built-in wardrobes and plush bedding, while the primary suite includes a private terrace, perfect for morning coffee or evening relaxation. The open-plan living area, bathed in natural light, connects effortlessly with the fully equipped kitchen and dining space, complemented by a cozy fireplace and comfortable seating, making it ideal for both short and long stays.

The home’s standout feature is its expansive rooftop terrace, offering breathtaking mountain views, elegant lounge seating, and sunbeds for soaking up the sun. Whether dining al fresco or unwinding under the charming archways, this outdoor space provides a peaceful retreat. Within the secure gated community, guests have access to a beautifully maintained swimming pool with sun loungers and shaded areas, surrounded by lush gardens that enhance the tranquil ambiance. With three modern bathrooms, including a stylish ensuite in the master bedroom, comfort and convenience are prioritized. Privacy and security are paramount, ensuring a worry-free stay.

Perfectly positioned just minutes from Puerto Banús, this four-bedroom townhouse is within easy reach of high-end shopping, beach clubs, and the world-famous marina. The Golden Mile offers an array of fine dining and exclusive boutiques, while Nueva Andalucía’s Golf Valley boasts prestigious courses such as Los Naranjos, Las Brisas, and Aloha Golf Club. The surrounding area is filled with top-rated restaurants, stylish cafés, and vibrant markets, making it an exceptional destination for those looking to experience the best of the Costa del Sol. Whether seeking relaxation, adventure, or cultural exploration, this home provides the perfect setting for an unforgettable stay.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.
