About the Property

Exquisite Beachfront Mansion with direct beach access!

An exceptional villa on a very large plot with direct access to the popular Hippopotamus Beach and South Beach Restaurant, located in one of the best areas east of Marbella, close to many golf courses and the popular 5-star Los Monteros Hotel. The mansion enjoys spectacular unobstructed views over the sea, to Gibraltar, the African coast, and mountains. The villa is 700m2 and built on a plot of 5000m2.

Villa Majestic enjoys wonderful top-quality marble floors, wooden beamed ceilings, and is fitted with A/C hot & cold throughout, IPTV systems in all bedrooms, an interior/exterior alarm system, and an “intelligent” illumination system in the garden.

This fantastic villa offers a 28-meter swimming pool with massage, jet stream, automatic self-cleaning system, and electric cover. Optional pool heating has an additional weekly cost. There is a 5-seater heated Jacuzzi next to the pool. On one side of the villa are the manicured gardens and on the other side there is an 18-hole putting green designed by the professional golfer, Manuel Jimenez.

The large pergola/bar area includes a BBQ, and a complete Bulthaup kitchen with Gaggenau equipment, including a fridge/freezer, ice-making machine, and a dishwasher. Surrounding the pool and on the terraces, you will find an array of outdoor lounge areas and sun loungers that can easily cater to up to 20 people.

The main entrance level consists of an elegant hall, a large living room with a fireplace, a guest bathroom, a dining room, and a fully fitted kitchen & laundry room (with double washer and dryers). There are 3 double bedrooms on this floor, two sharing a bath and one ensuite.

From the living room, you can walk straight out to the wonderful terrace, partially covered, along the length of the salon with staircases down to the pool area and vast garden.

A beautiful staircase leads to the first floor. This floor consists of a large master bedroom suite that offers a seating area, dressing room, private terrace and an independent marble bathroom with a 2-seater jacuzzi bath and 2 separate showers.

Also on this level are 3 more double bedrooms all with ensuite bathrooms and terraces with fantastic views from every room.

There are two parking areas for a total of 9 cars.

Marbella and Puerto Banús are a 15/20 minute drive away, both famous for international restaurants, designer boutiques and the ports berth some of the finest yachts in Europe. There are many golf courses to choose from to include Sotogrande, San Roque and Valderrama which hosted the Ryder Cup and Volvo Masters. The beaches here are the finest on the Costa Del Sol. All this only a 30-minute drive from Malaga airport.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.

We look forward to welcoming you.
