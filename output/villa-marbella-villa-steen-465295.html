About the Property

Beautifully renovated villa at walking distance to the beach with all the amenities for the perfect holiday; Pool, Outdoor Jacuzzi, Gym, Games Room with Pool Table etc

This elegant, fresh, and contemporary villa is situated in a very privileged location of Marbella, a stone’s throw from all amenities. Villa Steen has it all for the perfect holiday! Set back from the hustle and bustle, but a quick walk or short drive from all amenities and stocked with state-of-the-art fixtures and fittings. The home is filled with fantastic features such as a games room, jacuzzi, chill-out lounge, home gym and more! This is the perfect choice to come and enjoy the luxurious Marbella lifestyle with your every whim catered for.

The ground floor welcomes you to an open-plan living space finished with a fully equipped kitchen with a breakfast bar, a dining table, and a lounge area with a large Smart TV. Large sliding doors give seamless access to the wonderful terrace, garden and pool. The villa has ample space around the private pool full of sunbeds, coffee lounges, a barbecue area and outdoor dining areas, stairs up to a jacuzzi, and finally, an outdoor bar. The plot is lined by hedges and trees, providing unmatched privacy and tranquillity.

Inside, the soft-toned furnishings, subtle and smooth lines, top-of-the-range fixtures and good use of lighting and natural light provide this villa with a luxurious, spacious and relaxing ambience. On the ground level, there is a double bedroom with a private bathroom.

Upstairs, you find the Master Bedroom Suite that has an ensuite bathroom with a bathtub and large rain shower. There is access to the upper terrace overlooking the pool and garden. Here there is chill-out lounge furniture for you to kick back on.

There are 2 further double bedrooms on this level, both of which have the utility of one additional bathroom between them.

The highlight of Villa Steen is the variety of entertainment and recreational options available to guests. There is a fabulous Wine Cellar, an absolutely gorgeous dark wood pool table, a well-fitted home gym with various equipment to keep your body nifty while on your holiday. These, alongside the aforementioned perks of the home, tie it all together in pristine fashion to make Villa Steen as complete a home as you could wish for.

Additional features include Sonos speakers, high-speed wifi throughout, air conditioning, and not to mention, views from above stretching as far as the surrounding mountains! Please note there is street parking

Villa Steen is extremely well located. Only 8 minute’s walk to Puerto Banús and the beaches a little stretch further, it is also only 300 metres away from the popular Centro Plaza shopping centre, home to the Saturday market.

This luxury 4-bedroom villa promises to provide an unforgettable holiday experience. Don’t forget to enquire about our concierge services. We look forward to welcoming you!

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.
