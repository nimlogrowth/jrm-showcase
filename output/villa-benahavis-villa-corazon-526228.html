About the Property

Villa Corazon is a luxurious retreat in the prestigious El Herrojo gated community in Benahavis, offering a perfect blend of modern sophistication and natural beauty. This stunning three-level villa features five spacious bedrooms, each with an ensuite bathroom for optimal comfort and privacy. The master suite is a true sanctuary, boasting a walk-in closet and dual bathrooms—one for him and one for her. Designed with a charming Spanish aesthetic yet renovated with a sleek Scandinavian spirit, the villa seamlessly combines traditional warmth with contemporary elegance. An indoor patio fills the home with natural light, enhancing the open and airy ambiance, while the living and dining areas provide an inviting space for both relaxation and entertaining.

The villa’s private garden is a tranquil escape, offering breathtaking panoramic views of La Concha mountain, the Mediterranean Sea, and the fairways of La Quinta Golf. The beautifully landscaped outdoor space includes a large private pool, ideal for cooling off on warm days, while the terrace and outdoor dining area create a perfect setting for al fresco meals and gatherings. The master bedroom offers some of the most spectacular views, making it an exceptional vantage point to appreciate the beauty of the surrounding landscape.

Ideally situated, Villa Corazon offers the perfect balance between exclusivity and convenience. Just five minutes from Nueva Andalucía and a fifteen-minute drive to Puerto Banús and the Golden Mile, it enjoys close proximity to fine dining, boutique shopping, and world-class golf courses. Located frontline to La Quinta Golf, it is a dream for golf enthusiasts. As part of a high-end gated community with 24/7 security, the villa provides complete peace of mind. Private parking and a garage add further convenience, while generous terraces and modernized interiors enhance its luxurious appeal.

Please note, we welcome families and couples only. We kindly ask that no groups under 30, hen, or stag parties make bookings. However, if you fall outside these criteria but are interested in booking, please reach out with details about your group, and we’ll be happy to consider your request.
