About the Property

Luxurious 3-Bedroom Apartment Near Puente Romano

Welcome to your dream getaway! This stunning 3-bedroom, 2-bathroom apartment, fully renovated in 2024 to the highest aesthetic standards, offers an unparalleled living experience in one of Marbella’s most coveted locations. Just 200 meters from the world-famous Puente Romano Resort, you'll find yourself steps away from Marbella's finest restaurants, bars, beach clubs, shops, and beaches, making this the perfect base for both relaxation and adventure.

Sophisticated and Modern Design: The apartment features an open design kitchen, perfect for entertaining and culinary delights. Adjacent to the dining area, an interior steamer creates a tranquil, zen-like ambiance, making every meal a serene experience. The living space flows seamlessly onto a terrace equipped with an outdoor fireplace, ideal for cozy evenings with a view.

Exquisite Comfort: Each of the three bedrooms is designed with comfort and style in mind, offering a serene retreat after a day of exploring Marbella. The two bathrooms are modern and luxurious, featuring high-end fixtures and finishes that ensure a spa-like experience every day.

Breathtaking Views: The terrace overlooks the charming pool area and lush gardens, providing a picturesque setting for morning coffee or evening relaxation. The complex, though relatively small, offers an intimate and exclusive atmosphere, enhancing the overall sense of tranquility and privacy.

Prime Location: Situated just a stone's throw from the Puente Romano Resort, you’ll be at the heart of Marbella’s vibrant lifestyle. Enjoy gourmet dining, chic beach clubs, upscale shopping, and the stunning beaches, all within a few minutes' walk. Despite its central location, the apartment offers a peaceful haven where you can unwind and escape the hustle and bustle.

This luxurious apartment is the epitome of elegance and convenience, making it the perfect choice for discerning travelers seeking a high-standard, aesthetically pleasing retreat. Whether you're planning a family vacation, a romantic getaway, or a solo adventure, this apartment offers everything you need for an unforgettable Marbella experience. Welcome to your luxury escape in Marbella's best location!
