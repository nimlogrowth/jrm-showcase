About the Property

Welcome to Villa Palm Ridge, an extraordinary estate nestled in the prestigious urbanization of El Paraíso Alto, where luxury and opulence meet in a breathtaking 32,000 square meter plot of land.

This unique property boasts a sprawling 2,000 square meters of built size, offering a truly grand and majestic experience for those seeking a luxurious short-term rental in the heart of the Costa del Sol. Just a short 15-minute drive from Puerto Banús and a scenic 30-minute journey to Sotogrande, Villa Palm Ridge promises an unrivaled blend of convenience and serenity.

As you approach this magnificent villa, you'll be welcomed by an awe-inspiring entrance, complete with a grand driveway adorned with elegant water features. The opulent style of the villa is evident from the very first glance, promising an unforgettable stay in an exclusive and distinctive setting.

One of Villa Palm Ridge's most alluring features is its commanding south-west facing position, ensuring abundant sunshine throughout the day. From the spacious terrace with private pool, you'll be treated to sweeping open views of the Mediterranean Sea, the Paraiso golf course, and the lush valley below, creating a picturesque backdrop for your stay.

For the active traveler, Villa Palm Ridge offers a private tennis court, complete with rackets and night lighting for exhilarating matches under the stars. A small gym and a private Finnish sauna provide opportunities for relaxation and rejuvenation, while the entertainment room offers a perfect blend of productivity and leisure, featuring an office space, pool table, and a comfortable TV area.

With 9 luxurious bedrooms, each uniquely color-coded and accompanied by its own en-suite bathroom, multiple living room areas and 2 kitchens, Villa Palm Ridge is perfectly suited for large groups or families seeking both privacy and togetherness.

Outdoor living is a hallmark of this villa, with a spacious pool and terrace area offering stunning panoramic views. The pool includes a diving board, and nearby, a jacuzzi awaits, providing an idyllic setting to watch the sun dip below the horizon.

Throughout the meticulously landscaped gardens, you'll discover various seating areas, perfect for relaxation, al fresco dining, or simply taking in the beauty of the surroundings. As night falls, the garden comes alive with enchanting uplighting, creating a magical ambiance for unforgettable evenings.

The property places a premium on safety, featuring a top-tier security system for your peace of mind.

Villa Palm Ridge is a haven of luxury and tranquility, where every detail has been thoughtfully designed to ensure an unforgettable short-term rental experience. Whether you seek adventure, relaxation, or both, this villa offers the perfect retreat for your Costa del Sol getaway. Book your stay at Villa Palm Ridge and indulge in the epitome of Mediterranean living.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.
