About the Property

Step into the beating heart of Marbella’s at Casa El Dorado and lose yourself in its glamorous vibes. Chic and conveniently located, this immaculate apartment will place you within easy reach of some of the city’s top attractions while its charming surroundings and exquisite interiors offer the perfect hideaway for a family holiday. If you’re looking for a breezy, sunlit haven and want to experience the best of the Costa del Sol in style and luxury, this is, without doubt, your place to be.

The ultimate in style and comfort, Casa El Dorado has it all. Completely renovated to the highest Scandinavian standards with the latest mod cons and contemporary furnishings and finishes, combining natural woods and stone with the most current fixtures, this holiday apartment lacks for nothing.

Casa El Dorado is outfitted with an open-space living room incorporating a modern, fully-equipped kitchen—with a sleek island by the dining area — as well as a breezy living room, magnificent alfresco dining. This is continued on the terrace which overlooks the garden and pool and features an outdoor dining table for 6.

There are 3 bedrooms. The Master has a double bed and a separate walk-in closet. The second bedroom has 3 beds; The 3rd bedroom has a single bed that is a Nest bed, meaning a 5th bed can be made up for a child. There are 2 separate bathrooms.

Outside in the condominium swimming pool area, you will love its tranquil atmosphere, or swimming in the pool’s clear waters, is also perfect for your kids as it has a children’s pool!

The location of our apartment is perfect, close to it all yet in a quiet area. Puerto Banus is only a 15 minutes walk away, so is the beach. You have a few restaurants and bars within less than 5 minutes walk, along with a supermarket. Marbella Old Town is 10 minutes drive away, and you have 4 world class golf courses within less than 5 minutes drive (Aloha, Las Brisas, Los Naranjos, and La Quinta).

From its convenient location in Nueva Andalucía, you’ll have quick and easy access to so many of La Costa del Sol’s sought-after attractions, beaches, avant – garde cuisine, natural wonders, mountain-top white-washed villages, and more. Marbella’s warmer microclimate is an excellent perk of visiting the south of Spain, so you can hit the beach even in winter if you wish. Spotless skies and relaxing strolls along the shore can turn your holidays into a magnificent joyous break from work.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.
