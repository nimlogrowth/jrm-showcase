About the Property

Welcome to your private oasis in Guadalmina Alta — a stunning, single-level luxury villa where modern design, panoramic views, and complete comfort come together.

Spread across one elegant floor, this 5-bedroom, 5-bathroom home offers a seamless blend of indoor–outdoor living, highlighted by a long, beautifully designed pool that frames spectacular views over the sea and surrounding valley. The villa is bathed in natural light, with clean architectural lines and a spacious layout that makes every corner feel open and inviting.

This villa enjoys an unbeatable location — just a 5-minute drive from the nearest beach, allowing guests to enjoy the perfect balance between a private retreat and easy access to Marbella’s coastline. Spend the day by the sea and return home to complete peace and privacy.

One of the standout features of this property is the independent guest suite — perfect for visitors, older children, or staff who appreciate extra privacy. All bedrooms are generously sized with en-suite bathrooms, ensuring maximum comfort for larger families or groups.

Outside, the expansive terrace and pool area create an idyllic spot for sun-soaked days and unforgettable sunsets, while the peaceful residential setting provides total tranquillity just minutes from Marbella’s best amenities, golf courses, and beaches. Street parking is available directly outside the villa.

This home is ideal for families or groups seeking a refined, contemporary getaway in one of Marbella’s most sought-after neighbourhoods.

Please note, we welcome families and couples only. We do not accept groups under 30, nor hen or stag parties. If you fall outside this category but would like to book, please send us an inquiry with more details about your group, and we can consider your request.

We look forward to welcoming you!
