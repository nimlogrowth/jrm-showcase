About the Property

Welcome to Villa Jaz, an elegant and spacious 6-bedroom, 5.5-bathroom villa nestled in a residential enclave in Nueva Andalucia, just minutes from both Marbella and Puerto Banus. Designed with comfort, style, and connection in mind, this villa is the perfect setting for families or groups looking to experience the best of the Costa del Sol. From its sun-drenched garden and expansive pool to its inviting living spaces and thoughtfully designed interiors, Villa Jaz offers a harmonious blend of indoor luxury and relaxed outdoor living.

Step inside to discover a bright and open layout, where modern design meets warm Mediterranean charm. The fully equipped kitchen is a dream for entertaining, complete with sleek surfaces, ample prep space, and not one—but two wine fridges, ideal for stocking your favourite selections during your stay. Multiple dining and lounge areas offer flexibility for both group gatherings and quieter moments, and a guest toilet adds additional convenience. The six generously sized bedrooms are spread across the home to provide a sense of privacy and personal space for everyone, with a mix of en-suite and shared bathrooms finished to a high standard with elegant fixtures and clean, modern aesthetics.

Outside, the villa truly comes to life. The large private garden wraps around a beautiful swimming pool, framed by lush greenery and designed for full days of sunbathing, swimming, or al fresco dining under the stars. Whether you’re starting your morning with a quiet coffee on the terrace or winding down after a beach day with drinks by the pool, every corner of the outdoor space invites relaxation. Guests will love the ping pong table for a touch of friendly competition, while sun loungers and shaded lounge areas ensure total comfort throughout the day.

Villa Jaz enjoys a prime location with excellent proximity to amenities. Supermarkets, cafés, tennis courts, and top-rated restaurants are all nearby, while the golden beaches of Marbella and the glamour of Puerto Banús are just a short drive away. You’ll also be perfectly positioned to take on some of the Costa del Sol’s finest golf courses. Whether you’re after adventure, relaxation, or a bit of both, Villa Jaz is the perfect base for an unforgettable Mediterranean escape.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.
