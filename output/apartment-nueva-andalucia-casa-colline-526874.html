About the Property

This stunning west-facing penthouse is set within the prestigious Las Tortugas de Aloha urbanization in Nueva Andalucía, Marbella. Spanning two beautifully designed levels, it offers a perfect mix of modern comfort and elegant style, framed by breathtaking sea and mountain views. Its private and secluded setting, combined with expansive terraces and open-plan interiors, makes it an ideal retreat for those seeking peace, luxury, and sun-soaked living in one of Marbella’s most sought-after areas.

The penthouse features three spacious bedrooms and three elegant bathrooms, offering a bright and inviting living environment throughout. Designed with both relaxation and entertainment in mind, the home includes a fully equipped kitchen, stylish outdoor lounge areas, a BBQ setup, and a standout private rooftop gym—perfect for those who want to stay active while enjoying the views. The west- and south-facing terraces provide plenty of space to unwind or dine al fresco, while high-end finishes and thoughtful details enhance the overall experience of luxury living. On request, we can provide two extra single beds.

Beyond the residence itself, the location provides unbeatable access to some of Marbella’s finest attractions. Situated just minutes from the beaches and vibrant marina of Puerto Banús, you’re also close to the Aloha restaurant strip, world-class golf courses including Las Brisas and Los Naranjos, and the shopping, nightlife, and charm of both Marbella town and the Golden Mile. Whether you're here to golf, dine, or simply soak in the Costa del Sol lifestyle, this penthouse offers the perfect base for an unforgettable stay.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.
