About the Property

Gran Marbella Seafront Residence - Beachside with Sea Views!

Introducing a charming 2-bedroom apartment nestled within the prestigious and perfectly situated Gran Marbella complex. Sitting in the heart of Marbella, this cozy retreat offers an unparalleled coastal living experience.

As you step into the apartment, you're greeted by an atmosphere of soothing comfort. The apartment features a spacious layout with fresh colours, furnishings and decor, creating a stylish yet inviting ambiance.

The apartment features 2 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms. The master bedroom steps out onto the balcony and practically allows you to wake up with the view of the Mediterranean. The second bedroom is more designed for the kids featuring a bunk bed with a pull out bed and one additional single bed.

The highlight of this stunning residence is its private terrace, where you can unwind and soak in the Mediterranean sunshine while enjoying breathtaking views of the communal gardens, sparkling swimming pool, and the azure waters of the Mediterranean sea.

Gran Marbella Seafront Residence boasts a prime location as the complex offers direct access to the picturesque paseo, a charming promenade that leads directly to the beach. Whether you're in the mood for a leisurely stroll along the coastline or a refreshing dip in the sea, the convenience of beachfront living is right at your doorstep.

Surrounded by an array of shops, bars, cafes and restaurants, Gran Marbella Oasis offers easy access to a vibrant dining and entertainment scene. Explore the culinary delights of Marbella's renowned restaurants or indulge in a shopping spree at nearby boutiques and markets.

With its unbeatable location, number of amenities, and captivating views, Gran Marbella Marina is the perfect choice for those seeking a fun by day and cozy by night coastal retreat in Marbella.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.

We look forward to welcoming you!
