About the Property

Welcome to Hacienda del Sol, a charming beachside retreat located just a short walk from the shoreline and all essential amenities in Estepona’s desirable New Golden Mile. This inviting 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom apartment offers a bright and comfortable space ideal for both short getaways and longer stays. This home combines coastal charm with functional living in a peaceful, prime location.

The apartment features two well-sized bedrooms and two bathrooms—one with a bath/shower combo, and the other with both a bath and a proper walk-in shower. The open-plan layout flows into a cozy lounge area that shares its entrance with a professional shared office space, making the front lounge area a unique social hub for residents and co-workers alike. The interiors are filled with natural light and neutral tones, providing a clean and relaxing atmosphere.

With supermarkets, cafés, restaurants, shops, and the beach all within easy walking distance, everything you need is right at your doorstep. Whether you're soaking up the sun, working remotely, or heading out for a seaside dinner, this location offers the perfect blend of convenience and laid-back living on the Costa del Sol.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.
