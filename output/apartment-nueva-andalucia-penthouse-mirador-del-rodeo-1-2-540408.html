About the Property

Welcome to Penthouse Mirador del Rodeo 1.2, a stylishly renovated 4-bedroom, 3-bathroom duplex penthouse tucked between Puerto Banús and San Pedro. With tasteful design, generous space, and a spectacular rooftop setup, this home is ideal for families or groups looking to relax in comfort while staying connected to Marbella’s most iconic destinations.

The penthouse is split across two spacious levels, featuring bright interiors and refined finishes throughout. The main living area flows seamlessly to a covered terrace, perfect for casual lounging or morning coffee. Each bedroom is well-appointed and designed with relaxation in mind, while the three sleek bathrooms (including en-suites) offer modern comfort and convenience.

What sets this penthouse apart is its expansive rooftop terrace—an entertainer’s dream. Outfitted with a covered dining area, outdoor kitchen, wine fridges, and built-in BBQ, it’s the perfect space to host, dine, and unwind under the sun or stars. Stretch out on one of the sun loungers, fire up the grill, and take in the relaxed Marbella lifestyle in true style.

Set within a secure complex, guests also have access to a large communal pool and children’s pool, making it ideal for both relaxing days and fun with the family. Located next to Padel Real, Marbella’s go-to spot for fitness and padel, and within walking distance of the beach, a supermarket, cafés, and restaurants—everything you need is right at your doorstep.

Whether you’re here for sunshine, sport, shopping, or simply to recharge, Penthouse Mirador del Rodeo 1.2 delivers the perfect mix of luxury and location.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.
