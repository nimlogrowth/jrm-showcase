About the Property

Alcazaba Beach Penthouse is where Mediterranean dreams ascend to reality. Positioned frontline to the beach in the prestigious Alcazaba Beach complex, this exquisite 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom penthouse offers a hotel-suite experience with the comforts of a private home—and views that will leave you breathless.

Inside, the interiors speak fluent sophistication—where minimalist lines meet natural textures in a space that feels both contemporary and calm. The open-plan layout flows effortlessly from a designer kitchen to a light-filled living area, while both bedrooms are styled like luxury suites. Every corner is curated, and every detail considered.

But it’s the outdoor space that truly elevates this home. The expansive roof terrace offers panoramic views of the Mediterranean, a stylish dining area, a chill-out zone, and a built-in BBQ for sunset feasts under the stars. Whether lounging with a glass of rosé or hosting friends over open flame and ocean breeze, this terrace is your private stage to Estepona’s finest show.

As part of the exclusive Alcazaba Beach community, guests enjoy access to 7 swimming pools (including one heated), a gym, sauna, on-site restaurant, tennis and padel courts, and a children’s playground. With direct access to the beach promenade and everything you need within walking distance, this penthouse is more than a holiday home—it’s a beachfront lifestyle.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.
