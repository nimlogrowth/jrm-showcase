About the Property

Welcome to Villa Lulu, an exquisite retreat nestled in the residential neighbourhood of El Paraiso. This stunning new build boasts luxurious living across its expansive 6 bedrooms and 6 bathrooms, with 5 of them ensuites, offering unparalleled comfort and privacy for guests at the home.

Step inside Villa Lulu and discover a variety of entertainment with a dedicated games and TV room, featuring amenities such as a foosball and pool table. Stay active and energized in the convenience of your own home gym, thoughtfully fitted with simple yet effective equipment.

Experience alfresco dining at its finest on Villa Lulu's terrace, complete with a barbecue for unforgettable gatherings with loved ones. Unwind and soak up the sun on multiple terraces and balconies, each offering serene views of the surrounding beauty down as far as the sea.

Outside Villa Lulu, a generously sized garden provides ample space for relaxation and recreation, while the option to heat the pool ensures year-round enjoyment (pool heating has an additional cost).

Convenience is key at Villa Lulu, with private parking accommodating two cars and easy accessibility to nearby attractions. Explore the vibrant lifestyle of Puerto Banus and the charming allure of Estepona, both just a short drive away. Additionally, within a 15-minute drive, discover the heart of Marbella town.

For avid golf enthusiasts, local courses sit just moments away, while the golden sands of the beach are a mere 10-minute drive, offering endless opportunities for seaside bliss.

Indulge in the ultimate luxury lifestyle at Villa Lulu, where every detail has been meticulously crafted for your utmost enjoyment and relaxation.

We look forward to welcoming you!
