About the Property

This beautifully renovated 3-bedroom apartment is located in the sought-after El Dorado urbanisation in Marbella, just a short walk from the beach, shops, cafés, and restaurants. Designed with a refined Scandinavian aesthetic, the apartment combines soft natural tones, clean lines, and warm textures to create a calm and elegant coastal retreat.

The open-plan living and dining area is bright and welcoming, featuring a modern kitchen with high-quality appliances and a seamless flow onto the terrace. Large glass doors flood the space with natural light and lead out to a cozy private terrace with lovely sea views, perfect for relaxed breakfasts, sunset drinks, or long Mediterranean evenings. The terrace is fully furnished and includes a BBQ, making it ideal for outdoor dining.

The apartment offers three comfortable bedrooms, thoughtfully styled with neutral colours and soft furnishings for a restful night’s sleep. The bathrooms are modern and sleek, finished with natural stone textures and walk-in showers, adding a touch of boutique-hotel luxury.

Set within a well-maintained urbanisation and just minutes from Marbella’s coastline, this apartment is an excellent choice for guests looking to enjoy beachside living, walkable convenience, and stylish comfort—all in one of Marbella’s most central locations.

Perfect for families or couples seeking a relaxed yet well-connected holiday home by the sea.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.
