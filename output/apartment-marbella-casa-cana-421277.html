About the Property

A chic 2-bed duplex apartment with sea and mountain views in Puerto Banús.

Casa Caña enjoys amazing sea and mountain views, private parking, 3 swimming pools, and a padel court in a secure gated complex. The apartment is perfectly located with everything just a short stroll away. With some of Marbella's best shops and amenities at just a few minutes and Puerto Banus at a 10 min walk, this is one of the few unique places in Marbella where a car is not necessary.

The apartment has been elegantly refurbished, is beautifully styled in a contemporary Boho-chic style with top-notch furnishings and fixtures, and offers a bright and fresh feel.

Entering on ground level, you find the open-plan modern kitchen. This is fully equipped (including an oven, microwave, fridge/freezer, etc) and has a separate laundry area with the washing machine. There is a lounge area with sofas, a Smart TV, and a dining table for 4. This leads to the main terrace with amazing views of the La Concha Mountain down to the Mediterranean Sea. Here there is a chill-out corner, an outdoor dining table, and a gas BBQ.

There is a bedroom on this floor that offers twin 90cm beds, an ensuite bathroom (with a shower in the bathtub), and a private balcony with seating.

Downstairs is the Master Bedroom with a double bed and full bathroom and a private patio with seating. There is direct access to the communal areas from this patio.

There are individual aircon units in the bedrooms and high-speed wifi throughout the apartment.

Casa Caña is the perfect holiday getaway for those looking for a fresh holiday apartment with amazing views and all the necessities within walking distance! We look forward to welcoming you.
