About the Property

Tucked into the tranquil surroundings of Nueva Andalucía’s golf valley, Villa Serenis is a beautifully designed 5-bedroom, 4-bathroom retreat with an additional guest toilet, located just moments from the glamour of Puerto Banús. Offering panoramic views of La Concha and bathed in morning sunlight thanks to its east-facing orientation, the villa blends clean architectural lines with warm, inviting interiors—perfect for families or groups seeking a private escape without sacrificing access to Marbella’s best.

Inside, Villa Serenis impresses with its thoughtful layout and subtle luxury. Each bedroom is fitted with premium memory foam mattresses, a variety of pillow types—from supportive memory foam to softer alternatives—and cozy, elegant linens for a restful night’s sleep. The bathrooms throughout the home feature premium fittings, including modern walk-in showers, stylish tiling, and sleek, contemporary finishes that create a spa-like atmosphere. A separate guest toilet adds extra convenience for day-to-day use or when entertaining.

The main living spaces are bright and airy, seamlessly connecting the elegant lounge, dining area, and fully equipped modern kitchen. The villa features four strategically placed TVs, including a large-screen TV in the cinema room, another in the dining area, and two additional TVs in guest bedrooms, ensuring entertainment is always within reach.

Whether you're relaxing indoors or working remotely from the dedicated office, every detail of the home has been considered for comfort and convenience. Guests benefit from underfloor heating, a full WiFi MESH network, a phone/internet signal booster, and dimmable lighting throughout. Wellness features include a private gym, sauna, and a heated pool that can be enjoyed year-round.

Step outside to enjoy manicured gardens and tranquil spaces designed for downtime. The pool is surrounded by lush greenery and complemented by shaded dining and lounge areas—ideal for afternoon relaxation or evening gatherings. A carport with an electric vehicle charging station adds ease to your stay, while a secure alarm system offers added peace of mind.

Located in the serene Altos de Los Naranjos community, Villa Serenis is perfectly placed for golf lovers, with Los Naranjos Golf Club just around the corner. Puerto Banús and its designer shops, beach clubs, and marina dining are within a 10-minute drive, while Marbella’s charming Old Town and sandy beaches are also close by. Whether you're here for rest, reconnection, or a touch of luxury living, Villa Serenis delivers it all in a setting that feels like home.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.
