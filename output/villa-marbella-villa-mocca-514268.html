About the Property

Welcome to Villa Mocca! This villa combines modern comfort with relaxed living, making it an ideal choice for anyone looking to stay close to Marbella. Located beachside and just steps away from one of Marbella’s best beaches, Los Monteros Beach, you’ll also find vibrant restaurants, a supermarket (Aldi) within walking distance, and a boardwalk perfect for beach walks or bike rides. The area is home to some of Marbella’s best beach clubs, making it an unbeatable location for relaxation and fun.

With six spacious bedrooms, the villa is perfect for families or groups. Each room is designed with comfort in mind, offering cozy furnishings, lots of storage, and subtle Scandinavian designer furniture that adds a modern, elegant touch. The master bedroom includes an en-suite bathroom, a private balcony with gorgeous views, and a TV—a perfect spot to start or end your day. All six bathrooms come with modern showers, ensuring everyone has space and privacy. Additionally, there is a convenient guest toilet for added comfort.

The villa is newly built to Scandinavian standards and thoughtfully designed with bright, open living areas. Large windows let in plenty of natural light and provide views of the garden and pool. The kitchen is fully equipped with high-end appliances and plenty of counter space, making it easy to cook meals or enjoy a quick snack.

Outside, the villa’s garden is a true highlight. The overflow infinity pool creates a beautiful, tranquil setting where you can cool off or relax by the water with a good book. There is a fantastic new outdoor kitchen and BBQ area, paired with a seating area—perfect for hosting meals with family or friends. At night, the villa comes alive with light projections that add a unique and cool atmosphere.

For entertainment and exercise, the villa’s game room is located downstairs and includes a ping pong table, darts, and gym equipment. It’s a fun space that offers something for everyone, whether you’re in the mood for a game or a quick workout.

The villa is situated in a gated community with security cameras along the street, offering extra peace of mind during your stay. Additional amenities include air conditioning, fast Wi-Fi, and private parking, making your stay as easy and comfortable as possible. Whether you’re planning a vacation with family or a getaway with friends, this villa offers everything you need to relax, have fun, and make lasting memories by the sea.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.

We look forward to welcoming you.
