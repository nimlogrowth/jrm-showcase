About the Property

Sunshine Penthouse – A brand new Penthouse with Mountain & Sea Views!

Welcome to a lovely retreat in the brand-new Soul Marbella complex, perfectly positioned near the Santa Clara Golf Club on the east side of Marbella. This beautiful 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom penthouse apartment offers a harmonious blend of contemporary luxury and modern styling, with the added benefit of a fantastic Rooftop Terrace to soak in the aerial views of sunny Marbella!

As you step inside, you are greeted by a sophisticated and stylish atmosphere, with the spacious open-plan living area enjoying lots of natural light and elegant furnishings. The ultra-modern kitchen offers everything you need to cook up your favourite meals. The lounge area leads to the first terrace, providing outdoor dining and seating to enjoy the panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea.

From here you have a staircase leading to the Rooftop Terrace with outstanding views of the pools and gardens, on to the Med, with the backdrop of majestic mountains. There is an array of sunbeds, a dining table and BBQ, and a chillout zone – the perfect space to enjoy at any time of day.

The master bedroom has an en-suite bathroom that provides ample comfort, while the additional bedroom also has its own private bathroom, allowing for plenty of space and privacy for guests or visitors.

Beyond the allure of the apartment itself, Soul Marbella Penthouse shares all the features that the Soul Marbella complex offers; You will have access to an array of world-class amenities. Immerse yourself in the ultimate relaxation at the spa, take a refreshing dip in the indoor or outdoor pools, or maintain your wellness routine at the state-of-the-art private gym. There are beautiful communal spaces, walking paths and gardens. The apartment benefits from a secure underground parking space.

For golf enthusiasts, the complex neighbours the Santa Clara Golf Club which ensures endless opportunities to enjoy the sport against the backdrop of stunning scenery.

Soul Marbella Penthouse embodies the essence of luxury living in Marbella, offering an unparalleled lifestyle of comfort, convenience, and sophistication. A truly remarkable residence.

Within 10 minutes (by car), you can be in Marbella Old Town, and 15/20 minutes to Puerto Banus to the west and Fuengirola to the east. The beaches nearest to the Soul Marbella complex are the best in the region and have a variety of restaurants and bars to choose from.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.

We look forward to welcoming you!
