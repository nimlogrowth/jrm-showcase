About the Property

Welcome to Villa Zacaluca, a stunning contemporary Mediterranean villa designed for unforgettable holidays in the sun. This elegant three-level home combines modern luxury with relaxed outdoor living, creating the perfect setting for families, groups of friends, or couples seeking comfort, privacy, and style.

The villa features spacious open-plan living areas filled with natural light, high ceilings, and designer furnishings throughout. A fully equipped modern kitchen with premium appliances opens onto the dining and lounge areas, ideal for entertaining and socializing. The interior design blends soft neutral tones with bold accents, offering a refined yet welcoming atmosphere.

Outside, the villa truly shines. The private swimming pool is surrounded by sun loungers and lush greenery, offering a tranquil oasis for sunbathing and cooling off during warm days. For year-round comfort, the pool can be heated upon request for an additional cost. A stylish covered outdoor kitchen and bar area with barbecue provides the perfect space for long alfresco lunches and evening cocktails. Multiple terraces and seating areas allow you to enjoy both sun and shade at any time of day.

The landscaped garden is completely private and beautifully maintained, with palm trees, citrus trees, and elegant lighting creating a serene holiday ambiance. Whether relaxing on the shaded lounge terrace, enjoying a glass of wine by the pool, or hosting a sunset dinner, every corner of the property is designed for comfort and pleasure.

Inside, the villa offers beautifully appointed bedrooms with luxury bedding and modern ensuite bathrooms finished in marble and high-quality materials. Each space has been thoughtfully designed to ensure privacy, comfort, and restful sleep. Air conditioning throughout the property ensures a pleasant temperature year-round.

For guests who enjoy staying active, the villa also features a private gym. On the upper level, a jacuzzi on the balcony offers a wonderful spot to unwind while enjoying fresh air and peaceful surroundings — perfect for evening relaxation after a day in the sun.

Located in a peaceful and exclusive residential area, the villa offers both privacy and convenience. Beaches, golf courses, restaurants, and shops are just a short drive away, making this the ideal base for exploring the Costa del Sol while enjoying total relaxation at home.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.
