About the Property

Welcome to your private luxury villa on the Costa del Sol, where contemporary architecture, elegant design and exceptional outdoor living come together to create an unforgettable stay.

This newly built four-bedroom villa has been designed with both comfort and style in mind, offering generous space for families and groups who want to enjoy Marbella in a more exclusive and relaxed setting. Each bedroom features its own en-suite bathroom, ensuring privacy and convenience for every guest, while the lower level includes a high-quality sofa bed with a real mattress, comfortably accommodating two additional guests. One of the bathrooms also features a bathtub with jacuzzi function, adding an extra touch of relaxation.

From the moment you enter, the villa impresses with its clean lines, high ceilings and beautifully curated interiors. The open-plan living space is filled with natural light, with large floor-to-ceiling windows that seamlessly connect the indoor areas with the outdoor terraces and garden. The atmosphere is modern yet warm, creating a perfect balance between luxury and comfort. Underfloor heating throughout the villa ensures year-round comfort, while a full domotic system allows for easy control of lighting and climate. A high-quality Sonos sound system is available in the living area, outdoor terrace and master bedroom, creating the perfect atmosphere whether you’re relaxing indoors or enjoying the outdoor spaces.

The outdoor area is where this home truly stands out. Set within a private garden, the impressive 12-metre swimming pool becomes the centrepiece of your stay, ideal for long swims, relaxing afternoons in the sun, or spending quality time with friends and family. The covered terrace and lounge area offer a shaded retreat, while the fully equipped outdoor kitchen and BBQ create the perfect setting for long, relaxed dinners outdoors. For a bit of fun, the garden also features an outdoor ping pong table, perfect for both adults and children.

Upstairs, the rooftop terrace provides another level of luxury. Here you can unwind in the jacuzzi, enjoy a drink in the lounge area, or simply take in the open views and sunshine that define the Costa del Sol lifestyle. Whether it’s a peaceful morning or a sunset evening, this space is designed for pure relaxation.

The villa also features a spacious basement level dedicated to entertainment and wellbeing, including a stylish TV lounge, a bar area and a workout space equipped with a Technogym bench, making it ideal for both relaxing and staying active during your stay.

For peace of mind, the property is equipped with a modern alarm and camera system connected to a central security service.

For guests visiting outside the peak summer months, the pool can be heated during April, May, June, September and October, allowing you to enjoy the outdoor areas even during cooler periods.

Ideally located in a quiet and well-connected area, the villa is just a short walk from shops, restaurants and golf courses. The beach is only a few minutes’ drive away, while Puerto Banús and San Pedro can be reached in around 10 minutes, giving you easy access to some of the most sought-after destinations on the coast.

This villa is perfect for those looking for a modern, spacious and stylish retreat with premium features and a true indoor-outdoor lifestyle. Whether you are here to relax, entertain or explore Marbella, this is a place where you can fully switch off and enjoy every moment.

Please note that we welcome families and couples only, no groups under 30, and no hen or stag parties. Should you fall outside of this category but wish to book nonetheless, please send us an enquiry detailing a bit more about yourself and the group that you will be travelling with so we can request this for you.

We look forward to welcoming you!
